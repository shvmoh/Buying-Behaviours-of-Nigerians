# Buying-Behaviours-of-Nigerians-

*This dataset captures *consumer buying behavior* across different states and age groups in Nigeria.
It contains responses from shoppers describing:*

•⁠  ⁠*Demographics:* Gender, Age Range, Location.

•⁠  ⁠*Shopping Habits:* Frequency, Platforms used, Active vs. Occasional shoppers.

•⁠  ⁠*Financial Behavior:* Monthly spend, spend category, average spend.

•⁠  ⁠*Preferences:* Payment method, product types, buying decision factors.

### Total Columns: (12)
### Total Respondents: (107)

## Data Dictionary
Illustrations of each columns and their descriptions respectively:
                    
*Gender*: Male / Female                              
*Age Range* :18–25, 26–35, 36–45                        
*Location* :State where shopper resides 
*Shopping Frequency* :Number of purchases per month 
*Active Shopper*: “Frequent” or “Occasional”
*Average Spend Monthly*: Total monthly shopping spend (₦)        
*Monthly Spend Category*: High, Medium, Low                       
*Shopping Platforms*: WhatsApp, Jumia, Instagram, Physical Store  
*Preferred Payment Method*: Cash, Transfer, Card           
*Buying Decision Influence*: Ads, Friends, Discounts, Quality      
*Product Bought Frequently*: Food, Groceries, Electronics, Beauty, Fashion
